what's new?

  • The lunch menus are going to change!

    In an effort to be healthy, the Loundoun County (LCPS) have decided that they will be changing the school lunch menus! Children learning from home due to the pandemic, will be allowed to come to their schools and grab their lunch bags. While the students in the school building will have to grab their lunches from the lunch ladies.

  • Change in the bell schedule!

    The Willard middle school has decided that students will follow the new bell schedule due to the snow days. There will be a 2 delay in the bell schedule and the students will arrive to their classes 5 minutes early to get ready for class.
